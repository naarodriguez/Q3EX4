⎯⎯ ୨ Boracay ୧ ⎯⎯

photo taken in 27/12/22 by Nicole Rodriguez ❀
───✱*.｡:｡✱*.:｡✧*.｡✰ ───
Boracay is one of the most beautiful beaches in the Philippines. It an island located in Malay, Aklan. Boracay island is divided into three stations, station 1, station 2, and station 3, although the furthermost side is also now called station 0. Many tourists like to come to Boracay for it's beautiful sunsets, lively area, and for its activities.
───✱*.｡:｡✱*.:｡✧*.｡✰ ───
On December 26, 2022, me and my family went on a Christmas holiday trip to Boracay. It was hot and windy when we arrive, although it felt relaxing. During our 3-day stay in Boracay, we had many activities such as helmet diving, kayaking, exploring, and so on. After our stay and on the way home, I really missed Boracay and its relaxing vibe.
